How To Beat The Game

Man VS World

Starting Off, You are a lone stickman on a tiny island fightng for your life and world domination. Your Set On an island with nothing but your wits and a revolver that doesnt empty. Unload all those bullets on anything that spawns and have no fear. Anything you see can and will attack so do not hold back.

Try Not To Be Hit

You have a specific amount of health which is shown at the top of your screen, with the representation of a red bar. Everytime an enemy weither it be the dragons, birds, or even eyebal hitting you they will deal damage to you and remove health at the top of your screen.

Enemies

Each of your enemies are different, as shown at the top of the page, different enemies will either fly at you attack you head on or even lay exploding eggs ontop of you, or they will even shoot lazers directly.
